# Differences between Re-Console+ and Simply Legacy

Both Simply Legacy and Re-Console+ are modpacks made with the intent to give players a vanilla-friendly Legacy Console Edition experience. <br>
What makes them different is feature scope:
- Simply Legacy aims to have an experience closest to the original Legacy Console Edition, while also preserving much of vanilla Minecraft's gameplay and improving performance
- Re-Console+ aims to have extra content to enhance the gameplay experience past the base Legacy Console experience, with visual enhancements and additional content

### Key

- ✅ Included
- ❌ Not included
- 📝 Planned

::: info Disclaimer
These charts mostly pertain to the Modrinth versions of the modpacks. The CurseForge versions have less features due to issues with the distribution of certain projects on the platform.
:::
