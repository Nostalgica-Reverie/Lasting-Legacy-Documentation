# 2000s Edition
It aims to recreate Title Update 9 but with an added twist of what if it released in the early 2000s. It's aims to replicate the graphics at the time of 6th Gen consoles.
![Main Menu with Mac OS X Titlebar](/docs/modpacks/2000s-edition/images/main-menu.png)
## Resource Albums
This adds Resource Albums to aim the feel of the 6th Gen consoles such as the Xbox, PlayStation® 2, GameCube, and DreamCast.
